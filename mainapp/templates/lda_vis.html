LDA Visualization

This graph visualizes the different topics identified by LDA in the dataset. Each bubble represents a topic, where the size indicates the frequency of the topic and the position shows its relationship with other topics. Hover over the bubbles to see more details about each topic.

Metric Slider: This slider controls the number of topics displayed on the graph. Moving the slider to the right increases the number of topics, while moving it to the left decreases the number. This helps you focus on fewer or more detailed topics.
Overall and Estimated Frequencies: The size of each bubble represents the frequency of the topic in the dataset. Larger bubbles mean the topic appears more often, while smaller bubbles mean it appears less frequently. The position of the bubbles shows how related the topics are to each other.
Interactivity Note: Hover over any bubble to see more details about that topic. The bars will show the most important words related to the topic, giving you a quick understanding of what the topic is about.
